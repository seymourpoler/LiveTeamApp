How does the chat service work?

You can chat with the whole team or individually.
  • Whole team chat: Every message you enter in the "Team Chat" is delivered to all the collegues connected at that time. Users connecting after you sent the message will not see it.
  • Chat individually: To chat individually locate your colleague's button in the "Team Monitor", and click on it. If she is on pomorodo, you can send the message but it won't be displayed until she finishes it. However she still can see messages sent to the whole team.

What do the colours mean in the Team Monitor?

  • Red: mate is concentrated, on pomorodo, please don't interrupt her until the end of the countdown.
  • Blue: mate is working on a task, but she can help you anytime you need it
  • Orange: mate is apparently taking a break.
  • Dark: there are more than one session opened at the same time with the same user account. This is not recommended, please do not open the application in several tabs or windows.
  • Changing colours: do colours change constantly with no sense? This means that the same user account is opened several times. From the same machine or from different machines.
